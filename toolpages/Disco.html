Fluxicon-Disco

The process mining technology in disco can automatically create smart flow diagrams of your process. All you need are event logs that are already on your IT systems. Because the tool work with this objective information, you no longer need to rely on belief or hearsay — you will know what's going on.

Pro's

• The best things about this software is its on screen coding.
• You can easily see related and family documents on the review screen, and can see how they've been tagged.
• Documents can easily be shared via a link sent through email, or a notification within the system.
• Navigation through the databases, ease of ingestion, quick and informed responses from customer support and project managers.

Con's

• There seam to be no proper function to list the doccument within the coding screen.
• The search functions are a bit convoluted. In an attempt to streamline the search functions, there are some glaring difficulties -- searching within an assigned batch is not as comprehensive as searching the entire database of documents and you cannot search by a specific batch.
• Cannot resize filter side box, hard to navigate through document families in non- reviewer mode, no options to include disco IDs on downloaded documents without batch printing.
