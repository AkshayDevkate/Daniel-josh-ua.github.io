MEHRWERK ProcessMining

MEHRWERK GmbH was founded in 2008 with the vision of implementing agile and user-friendly data analytics solutions in order to achieve acceptance and direct added value for its customers. The company has developed MEHRWERK ProcessMining (MPM) on the market-leading self-service BI platform Qlik Sense in order to offer comprehensive process mining functionality to enterprise customers.

Pro's

• The Qlik SenseR platform provides user-friendly analysis based on governed data discovery.
• With the Qlik Associative Engine it is possible to discover relations in even vast amounts of data by technically performing a many-to-many full outer join in the back-end.
• The tool provides powerful and interactive search, selection and filter functions due to a patented in-memory technology including compressed binary indexing, logical deduction and dynamic KPI calculation and aggregation.
• It provides price analyses, production controlling, e-commerce & web analyses, customer journey as well as process costs analytics.
