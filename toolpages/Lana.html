LANA Process Mining

Lana Labs GmbH is a leading provider of cloud based process mining software. The company enables organizations worldwide to automatically analyse business and production processes. LANA Process Mining includes a specially developed machine learning algorithm that determines data-based and automated savings and optimization potentials in business processes.The goal of Lana Labs is to make companies smarter, more efficient and faster through automated process analysis.

Pro's

• Process explorer is really good.
• Very engaged team with great customer care (taking into account needs).
• Strong tool for optimizing and monitoring processes of all kinds.
• The tool is great to collect different events from individual data sources and therefore enables to have a combined view on the full process.

Con's

• Its a bit difficult to understand what data format the timestamp needs to be in during Csv upload.
• Data gathering from the complex system landscapes is a bit difficult.
