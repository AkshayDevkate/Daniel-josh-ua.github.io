Minit

Minit is a process mining platform that allows businesses to gain data-based insights using its deep process analysis of your business workflows. The software helps you uncover insights that have the potential to drive efficiency across your organization and contribute greatly to the realization of your business goals.

Pro's

• Minit goes through tons of businesses processes to discover insights and create maps based on event logs and actions performed by your IT system. Using this information, Minit builds a very graphic visualization of all the variants of the process, helping you see the details you have never seen before.
• The system automatically evaluates your business processes and reveals vulnerabilities that you didn’t know existed, such as bottlenecks, unnecessary detours, or compliance issues. With such discoveries, you are able to determine the inefficiencies and make the necessary improvements to resolve processes that take up time and drive up costs.
• Collaboration is vital in improving your business processes and Minit lets you work with your colleagues seamlessly. You can take an interactive snapshot of the state of your analysis and make it viewable to your colleagues, who can then see your work and the steps that you did. They can then work on your analysis and continue your process or collaborate for better results.
• You have the option to deploy Minit as a standalone platform, on premises, or in the cloud, giving you the flexibility you need to implement the system based on your business requirements and preferences. Regardless of your choice of deployment, Minit is easy to set up and the whole process can be completed within a very short time and with little effort.

Con's

• A robust data treatment is needed for eventlogs before uploading it to Minit. This can bring a huge effort from analytic teams.
