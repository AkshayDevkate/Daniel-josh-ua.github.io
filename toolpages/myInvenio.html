Myinvenio

My-Invenio is a process mining tool that can help you understand and take a deep view at the insights of your real-time processes. So that you have a better idea about actual process efficiency and you can easily predict the future circumstances which may lead to unwanted conditions. I think this is the best thing because it always enables us to fix the root cause which is causing problem and help us to spend right in the technology

Pro's

• MyInvenio has had advanced capabilities for checking conformance and certification with predefined models and procedures.
• Most deployments of myInvenio are SaaS offerings.
• Cognitive Technology focuses as well on the depth of process functionality as on the breadth of operational functionality.

Con's

• Report generating is not as efficient(sometimes).
• The intial setup to capture data from the different data sources is quite complex.
• UI does not seem user friendly and looks little complex.
