Signavio Process Intelligence

Process Intelligence, powered by Signavio, conducts powerful, fact-based analysis of an organization’s business systems, helping to drive smarter decisions and reduce the time to implement changes that matter. Integrated with Infor Implementation Accelerators, Process Intelligence quickly identifies exceptions and opportunities for improvement via conformance checking of standard practices.

Pro's

• Easy to use and learn. This is fully web based software which helps to save the space on your system and helps to manage documents easily.
• It has different Notation languages (Archimate for Enterprise Architecture Diagrams) Dictionary which is extremely valuable in mapping the IT Systems and Data Objects in the Business Process maps.
• The Collaboration Hub feature makes the processes much more "alive" in your organisation by giving all employees and stakeholders the ability to view processes and additional related information.
• Easy to use SaaS Scalable - Does process modelling, simulation, workflow, and process mining.

Con's

• It does not provide proper connectivity with many back-end systems.
• You can share documents but cannot work on them at the same time.
• Missing Workflow function stored in maps makes is not usable for Reporting.
• Dictionary is to slow and Reporting limits (250 processes).
• Running a complex process might be difficult since the simulation might not handle the process and stop prematurely.
