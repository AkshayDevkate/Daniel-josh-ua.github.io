Logpickr Process Explorer

Logpickr Process Explorer 360 is one of the most robust process mining tools available. It enables you to analyze and optimize business processes. Also, it includes features like audit & compliance, custom dashboards, and compliance management.

Pro's

• Logpickr Process Explorer 360 allows you to understand end to end processes, regardless of the teams and software used.
• Logpickr Process Explorer 360 it is intended for all managers, whether experts or novices in Process Mining.
• The Logpickr process explorer visualization provides extremely relevant information about the processes and highlights improvement paths that cannot be detected elsewhere.
