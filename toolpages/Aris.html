Aris Process Mining

ARIS offers true business process transformation via tightly managed and controlled change. With ARIS, you will create, manage and analyze your end-to-end business processes, enabling operational excellence and giving all stakeholders the tools and process insights they need to make better decisions every

Pro's

• The AI-based ARIS Root Cause Miner automatically identifies root causes.
• Existing process models can be enhanced by real performance measurements and structural context information with a context-sensitive side-by-side visualization.
• ARIS Process Mining supports a fully governed transformation of business processes while involving all necessary stakeholders.

Con's

• Reporting and Mining is relatively slow.
• The visuals become much complicated if you're designing a new process
• It takes more time for Integrating with other tools
• All the capabilities of the tool is not available in one single interface (ARIS Connect, thick client)
